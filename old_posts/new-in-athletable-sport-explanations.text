# New in Athletable - Sport explanations

 - published: 2012-12-10 16:41
 - tags: athletable

A lot of offices play table tennis but not all of them might score it the same way. Is it games to 21 or games to 11? Or do you just score best of 5 games? It's up to you but it helps with stats if your office is consitent. In aid of that I've added sport explanations; a quick note that can be set for each sport to help players know how your organisation scores it.

![Sport explanations](media/sport-explanations.png):center

It's completely optional but use if it helps.
